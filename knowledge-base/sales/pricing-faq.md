# Sales Pricing FAQ

This fictional pricing FAQ helps the demo assistant answer business questions.

## Pricing Positioning

FIND AI is positioned as a lightweight company knowledge assistant for teams
that want local-first RAG without a complex enterprise search rollout.

## Demo Plan

The demo plan is for small teams testing the assistant locally. It includes the
Streamlit app, markdown knowledge base, local ChromaDB storage, and Ollama model
support.

## Team Plan

The team plan is intended for companies that want document upload workflows,
basic analytics, and support for multiple knowledge-base categories.

## Enterprise Plan

The enterprise plan is intended for customers that need access controls,
security review, deployment support, and custom integrations.

## Common Objection

If a buyer asks why FIND AI uses local models, explain that local inference can
reduce hosted model costs and help teams keep sensitive documents within their
own environment.
