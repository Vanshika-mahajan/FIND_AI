# Leadership And Operating Rhythm

FIND AI is organized as a small cross-functional team with lightweight planning
and frequent customer feedback.

## Leadership Team

- Vanshika Mahajan, Founder and Product Lead: owns product direction, user
  experience, and roadmap priorities.
- Aarav Mehta, Engineering Lead: owns backend architecture, retrieval quality,
  and local model integration.
- Naina Kapoor, People Operations Lead: owns onboarding, hiring coordination,
  employee policies, and team rituals.
- Rohan Iyer, Customer Success Lead: owns customer onboarding, support workflows,
  and feedback loops.

## Weekly Cadence

- Monday planning: confirm priorities, risks, and delivery goals.
- Wednesday product review: inspect UX, retrieval quality, and answer behavior.
- Friday demo: show shipped progress, customer learnings, and open questions.

## Decision Making

Most product and engineering decisions are made by the directly responsible
team lead. Decisions affecting customer data, security, or pricing require
review from Product, Engineering, and Customer Success.

## Communication Norms

- Document decisions in markdown so they can be retrieved by the assistant.
- Use short written updates before meetings.
- Prefer source-backed answers over memory when discussing policy or product behavior.
