# Company Overview

Replace this sample file with real company knowledge.

FIND AI helps teams search, understand, and answer questions over internal company documents.
The company RAG assistant should only answer from approved documents in this knowledge base.

## Supported documents

Good source files include company policies, product notes, onboarding guides, customer FAQs,
sales enablement notes, security docs, and process documentation.

## Source hygiene

Keep documents factual, current, and clearly named. Remove outdated content before ingestion
so the assistant does not retrieve stale answers.
