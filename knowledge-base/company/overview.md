# FIND AI Company Overview

FIND AI is a fictional company used to demonstrate a company RAG assistant.
The company builds tools that help teams search, understand, and answer questions
over internal documents using retrieval-augmented generation.

## Mission

FIND AI helps growing teams turn scattered company knowledge into reliable,
source-backed answers.

## Vision

The company wants every employee to find trustworthy internal information without
searching through long document folders, stale notes, or disconnected systems.

## Core Values

- Clarity: write and communicate in a way that makes work easier for others.
- Trust: cite sources, preserve context, and avoid unsupported claims.
- Speed with care: move quickly while protecting customer and company data.
- Practicality: prefer useful workflows over impressive but confusing features.

## Main Teams

- Product: defines the company knowledge assistant experience.
- Engineering: builds ingestion, retrieval, model orchestration, and app UI.
- People Operations: manages onboarding, policies, and employee programs.
- Sales: explains the product to prospective customers.
- Customer Success: helps customers onboard documents and improve answer quality.

## Current Focus

FIND AI is focused on building a local-first RAG system for company knowledge.
The current project uses markdown documents, ChromaDB, Sentence Transformers,
Ollama, and a Streamlit interface.
