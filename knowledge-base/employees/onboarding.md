# Employee Onboarding Guide

New employees at FIND AI follow a five-day onboarding plan focused on company
context, product understanding, and safe use of internal knowledge.

## Day 1

- Review the company overview and team structure.
- Set up local development tools.
- Install Ollama and pull the approved local model.
- Read the data handling policy before using customer documents.

## Day 2

- Learn the RAG workflow: ingest, retrieve, generate, cite.
- Run the Streamlit app locally.
- Ask test questions against the sample knowledge base.
- Review examples of good and poor source citations.

## Day 3

- Pair with an engineering or customer success teammate.
- Add a small markdown document to the knowledge base.
- Rebuild the vector index and verify that the assistant can retrieve it.

## Day 4

- Review product goals and customer use cases.
- Study support escalation guidelines.
- Join a product review meeting.

## Day 5

- Complete a small improvement task.
- Document what changed.
- Share one question that should be added to the evaluation set.

## Onboarding Owner

People Operations owns onboarding logistics. The hiring team owns role-specific
training and first-week tasks.
